Biography

Many people only view games as an activity to waste time and as a form of low brow entertainment, but they are so much more to me. They are how I’ve met long-time friends, stayed connected with family, and experienced stories in ways that other media cannot. The right game can change a person’s life, and that is what I aspire to create.
